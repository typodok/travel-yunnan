Our Journey

Meet Jan & Xianzi

We are a European-Chinese couple with a shared passion for discovering the authentic beauty of China, especially the breathtaking landscapes of Yunnan province.

Jan (pronounced "Yan"), originally from Europe, brings a former IT automation engineer's perspective to travel planning. His attention to detail and problem-solving skills ensure every journey is seamless and well-organized.

Xianzi, a professional photographer from China, captures the soul of each destination through her lens. Her artistic eye helps travelers see China in a new light, focusing on genuine moments and hidden beauty.

Together, we founded Yunjian Travel to share our love for off-the-beaten-path adventures, rural countryside, and the simple authenticity that makes China truly special.

What We Love

Our passions shape the experiences we create for travelers

Yunnan's Beauty

The diverse landscapes, ethnic cultures, and breathtaking clouds of Yunnan never cease to amaze us. It's where our story began.

Rural Countryside

We're drawn to the peculiar charm of China's countryside - terraced fields, ancient villages, and the genuine warmth of rural communities.

Authentic Nature

From misty mountains to crystal-clear rivers, we seek out the unspoiled natural wonders that showcase China's incredible diversity.

Local Cuisine

We believe food is the heart of culture. We love discovering and sharing authentic local dishes and the stories behind them.

Our Mission

We're passionate about showing travelers the real China they might not have heard about - beyond the tourist crowds and polished attractions.

Our experiences focus on:

  • Authentic interactions with local communities
  • Preserving and respecting cultural heritage
  • Sustainable tourism practices that benefit local economies
  • Creating meaningful connections between travelers and places

Through our journeys, we hope to share the humility and genuineness we've discovered in China's hidden corners, creating memories that last a lifetime.
