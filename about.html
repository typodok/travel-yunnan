Our Journey

Meet Jan & Xianzi

We are a European–Chinese couple who fell in love with exploring China together, especially the wild beauty of Yunnan.

Jan (pronounced "Yan") is from Europe and used to work in the IT industry. He has a knack for planning smooth and thoughtful journeys. For him, the real joy of travelling in China is discovering the charm that lies beyond the standard guidebooks.

Xianzi is a professional photographer with a keen eye for detail. She loves food and travel, and enjoys planning trips that are both comfortable and delightful. With her camera, she likes to capture unique and genuine moments—including the ones you’re in.

Together, we founded Yunjian Travel to share our love for off-the-beaten-path adventures, rural countryside, and the simple authenticity that makes China truly special.

What We Love

Our passions shape the experiences we create for travelers

Yunnan's Beauty

The diverse landscapes, ethnic cultures, and breathtaking clouds of Yunnan never cease to amaze us. It's where our story began.

Rural Countryside

We're drawn to the peculiar charm of China's countryside - terraced fields, ancient villages, and the genuine warmth of rural communities.

Authentic Nature

From misty mountains to crystal-clear rivers, we seek out the unspoiled natural wonders that showcase China's incredible diversity.

Local Cuisine

We believe food is the heart of culture. We love discovering and sharing authentic local dishes and the stories behind them.

Our Mission

We create one-of-a-kind travel plans that keep each journey colourful yet comfortable. We also help you connect with local culture in a genuine way, not just through the usual sightseeing. We love discovering the real charm and character of a place, the kind of details you won’t find in typical travel guides. And we make room for small, meaningful moments that turn a trip into something truly yours.
